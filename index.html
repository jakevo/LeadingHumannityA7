FEATURES OF CITIZEN SCIENTIST

  • ﻿﻿ CREATE NEW RESEARCH PROJECT
    You can design your own research project and execute it by uploading a google form that will serve as a sourvey.
  • ﻿﻿ CONTRIBUTE DATA TO EXISTING PROJECTS
    You can answer the sourveys other people have created for their projects.
  • ﻿﻿ ANALYZE DATA OF AN EXISTING PROJECT
    You can review the curent data for existing projects and provide feedback based on the hypothesis and the data for that particular project.
  • ﻿﻿ MONITOR MY PROJECTS
    With this feature you can view the data from other users answering the sourvey you created when you made a new project. You can also track other projects you are interested in and be able to view their data.
